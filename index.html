01 / About

Technology in service of people and the environment.

I translate complex scientific data into clear, accessible tools that help people explore information and make informed decisions.

I develop, maintain, test, and improve web-based applications at DOST–PAGASA. My work centers on interactive mapping, climate information, satellite rainfall, heat index, flood forecasting, and drought monitoring.

I work across front-end and back-end development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Leaflet.js, PHP, MySQL, Python, APIs, Linux, and AWS services including EC2 and S3. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from STI College Caloocan.
